Return Policy

We accept returns and exchanges within 30 days from the date you received your order. For orders within the Philippines, however, we will only accept returns up to 7 business days from the date of receipt.

Exchange requests outside of these grace periods will not be accepted except for extraordinary cases.

We do not offer cash refunds and our policy is a STORE COUPON REFUND ONLY Policy.

Unfortunately, due to the costs of handling, processing, shipping international orders, and payments, we cannot issue cash refunds for returns. We are happy to grant store credit in return for your newly purchased Skoop item that may not fit perfectly for the first time.p>

Note: We will issue store credit only for the value of the items returned. Shipping is non-refundable.

Aside from applying for a return within the grace period stated above, below are the other requirements for us to accept your return request:

1. Item/s were not purchased with a discount of 25% or more. These sales are considered final and cannot be returned

2. All return items must be unused and undamaged. Washing or altering the item/s in any way are considered damaging it. We suggest wearing it over other pieces of clothing when you are trying it on for the first time to avoid stains and damages

3. It must be returned in original condition, complete with its hang tag.

The buyer is responsible for all return costs—including shipping, duties, and taxes. These are not covered and will not be reimbursed by us.

Given this, please declare minimum shipment value for returned parcels. We reserve the right to refuse a return that has unpaid duties and taxes being charged to us.
